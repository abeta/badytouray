On 6th March 2015 Bady fought Danny Anderson at York Hall in Bethanl Green for the elimination for UKBU English Title & Bady had put on a show stopping event by stopping Danny Anderson in the second round winning by TKO. Martin Durrant is Bady's mandatory challenger and final step as they go head to head to fight for the UKBU English Light-Cruiserweight Title belt.
